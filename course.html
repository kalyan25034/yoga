Courses We Offer

We offer courses based on your Interest and Expertise. you can take part as LEARNER or TRAINER based on your skill.

for LEARNERS , we offer 3 levels of YOGA trainings.
after verifications of certificates , TRAINERS will be allotted with sessions and rewarded as per them.

Beginner

As a Beginner , you should always remember that YOGA is a journey not a race . be patient with yourself and respect your body limitations. seek guidance and practice regularly.

Intermediate

As a Intermediate practitioner, you may ready to explore more advanced poses but however one should always priortize his own safety and listen to your body . This course helps you get more awareness and opens you to different variations of YOGA.

Advanced

As a Advanced practitioner , you have reached profound level in physical dimensions of yoga. this course helps you to mastery in spiritual dimensions of YOGA.
